How

[ .$grav ]

GRAV is the force that holds the naafiri ecosystem together. It fuels a thriving economy that rewards stakers and incentivizes players, with its supply diminishing over time.

The GRAV token powers the metaverse, transforming heroes into yield-bearing assets.

Without GRAV, there is no naafiri.

learn more

[ .Heroes ]

Heroes (NFTs) are player-owned avatars that survived the Maelstrom and made it into the world of Zetyik

It could be a Tank, DPS or Healer class that lives on Ethereum, BNB Chain or Arbitrum. Players control Heroes to explore, battle and conquer to determine their destiny.

[ .Loot ]

Loot are powerful in-game items such as armor and weapons that play an integral role in your journey. Loot is equipped to your Hero to increase their Power and allow them to battle and defeat evermore powerful foes.

Loot does not only increase a Heroes in-game power but they also impact their earning potential on-chain.

[ .Resources ]

Resources are the in-game assets that players need to acquire and manage throughout their journey. From Gold and Celestial Power to Prestige XP and Achievement Points.

Effective resource management will be the key to a successful and powerful Hero.
